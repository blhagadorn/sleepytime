About Sleepytime

I provide in home sleep training in Chicago, as well as phone consult sleep training anywhere in the United States.

Some of the services that I provide include:

  • Gathering a complete intake of the current sleeping and behavioral patterns as well as the surrounding environment
  • Providing an age appropriate schedule for naps and night sleep
  • Educating and supporting parents
  • Troubleshooting potential issues and offering professional options to resolve them
  • Establishing healthy sleeping habits

Bio

I received my bachelor’s degree in nursing from Marquette University in 1989. My first nursing job was in labor and delivery, and I’ve been working with families, children, and infants ever since. When I started working as a newborn care specialist, I realized that the end result of my care should be babies sleeping well. My interest in sleep training grew and I pursued my certification as a consultant. I offer different methods depending on what is appropriate for the child and what the family’s wishes are. I work with singletons and multiples, ages four months to toddler. Previously I worked at Bliss Newborn Care.

When I am not working with families, my interests include spending time with my family, gardening, cooking, exercising, reflexology, and keeping up with the lives of my three adult children.
